About Me

Hello, I'm Matt!

21 year old Computer Science student, graduating in July 2016, with a passion for full-stack development and journalism.

For three years, I have been a member of Burn FM, the student radio station at the University of Birmingham. For two of those years, I served on the committee as webmaster, completely redesigning and restructuring the website for the betterment of the soceity, still in use today.

Also during my time at Burn FM, I have represented the society as a reporter, providing sports analysis for television broadcasts as well as web content. I have also had the opportunity to interview many modern sports stars, including Brendon McCullum and Chris Woakes.

Journalism

During my time at University, I discovered a passion for journalism. Working at the student radio station, I regularly wrote match reports, recorded commentaries and conducted interviews as part of an award winning sports team.

This allowed me to expand my knowledge and skills in writing, granting me the ability to concisely record events to paint an accurate picture in the future.

My reports have not just been limited to Burn FM, with the University of Birmingham Sports PR also using some of my work to report matches.

I also worked as a presenter, hosting my own radio show and providing sports analysis for Guild TV's flagship sports broadcast, xpLosION for 2 years.

This allowed me to develop skills in public speaking and presenting in general.

Links to both xpLosION appearances, and the archive of podcasts from my radio show, Version 2.0, can be found below.
